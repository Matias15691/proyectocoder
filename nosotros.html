About Us

RAGONE Ingenieria began its activities in 1991, providing high-level engineering and project management services to the industry. Our international experience and value-added engineering services, including metallurgical, lithium and cement/lime processes, mechanical, piping, civil, structural, electrical, automation and control engineering, as well as in project/construction management to the mining, cement/lime, grain/seed, manufacturing and infrastructure industries allow us to attend a growing and prominent international clientele. Our project management procedures have demonstrated high effectiveness to meet clients´ needs while achieving the demanding targets of their projects. These unique features have allowed RAGONE Ingenieria to be involved in the most relevant projects of the industries we attend, and to appropriately respond to our growing customers’ demands worldwide.

Company Policies

RAGONE Ingenieria complies with a Quality Policy with the aim of strengthening the commitment with its clients, collaborators, and suppliers. The present Quality Policy is based on the following principles: Satisfy in time, with precision and quality the wide spectrum of technical needs of our clients, involving appropriate and efficient solutions throughout all stages of their projects, as well as completing all the requirements relevant to the activity. Promote the training and continuous improvement of our professionals to achieve excellence in engineering and procedures, Continuously improve our internal processes by promoting the participation and commitment of our staff, implementing proven and appropriate work methodologies, as well as providing the necessary resources for the development of activities inherent to our company, Have a healthy, clean, tidy workspaces provided with the necessary technological tools for the efficient development of our activities in order to optimize productivity.
